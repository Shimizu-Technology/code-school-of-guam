## 1. Introduction

### Mission and Vision
- **Mission**: Provide high-quality, accessible coding education to equip the people of Guam with software engineering skills for the job market.
- **Vision**: Transform Guam into a tech hub by empowering residents with global software industry skills and real-world experience.

### Program Overview
Code School of Guam offers a comprehensive curriculum in full-stack software development with AI Engineering, focusing on:
- **Backend**: Ruby on Rails (API mode)
- **Frontend**: React.js
- **AI Engineering**: Python, OpenAI SDK, RAG with vector databases

The program includes:
- **Pre-Work (5 weeks)**: Self-paced learning with recordings and weekly check-ins
- **Live Classes (17 weeks)**: Interactive, instructor-led sessions conducted via Zoom
- **Optional Internship**: A 10-week internship to gain real-world project experience
- **Career Services**: Support in resume building, interview prep, and job search strategies

## 2. Admissions Process

### Eligibility Criteria
- **No Prior Experience Needed**: Open to those interested in software engineering, regardless of background
- **Class Attendance**: Must attend all scheduled classes
- **Technology Access**: Suitable laptop and reliable high-speed internet connection
- **Financial Commitment**: Ability to pay tuition via selected payment options

### Application Steps
1. **Application Form**: Complete the online form at codeschoolofguam.com
2. **Schedule an Interview**: An acknowledgment email will be sent upon application submission. Schedule a brief interview to discuss program details and fit.
3. **Enrollment**:
   - **Non-Refundable Deposit**: Pay a $500 deposit to secure a spot (applied toward tuition)
   - **Payment Selection**: Choose a payment option (full payment or installment plan)
   - **Onboarding**: Receive course materials, schedule, and access to platforms

### Payment Options

#### Full Upfront Payment
- **Discount**: $1,000 off the total tuition
- **Total Cost After Discount**: $9,000

#### Installment Plan
- **Structure**: Monthly payments over the course duration
- **Schedule**: First payment due by the Monday before pre-work begins. Subsequent payments due on the first Monday of each month.
- **Total Cost**: $7,500

### Installment Payment Terms
- **Commitment to Pay**: Students who choose the installment plan are required to pay the full tuition amount of $7,500, regardless of continued participation
- **Consequences of Non-Payment**:
  - If a payment is not made by the due date, a 3-day grace period will be provided
  - If payment is not received by the end of the grace period, the student will be removed from the program
  - Re-entry is only allowed upon full payment of any outstanding balance
  - Outstanding balances may be turned over to collections
- **No Refund for Partial Completion**: Students who withdraw or are dismissed after the second week of live classes remain responsible for the full tuition balance

## 3. Course Structure and Schedule

### Program Duration
- **Total Length**: 22 weeks (~5.5 months)
- **Pre-Work**: 5 weeks
- **Live Class**: 17 weeks
- **Weekly Class Time**: Approximately 17.5 hours live instruction

### Class Schedule
- **Live Class (Mon – Thu)**: 6:00 PM – 9:00 PM (ChST/GMT+10)
- Short breaks every 50 minutes

### Teaching Methods
- **Live Instruction**: Ruby on Rails, React.js, Python, and AI Engineering sessions
- **Hands-On Projects**: Real-world projects during class, including an AI-integrated mini-capstone
- **Pair Programming & Group Work**: Collaborative exercises to enhance learning
- **Code Reviews**: Regular feedback on code

### Assessments and Grading
- **Weekly Assignments**: 20%
- **Mid-course Quizzes**: 10%
- **Capstone Project**: 50%
- **Participation**: 20%

## 4. Technology Requirements

### Recommended Equipment
We highly recommend that students use a Mac for the duration of the program. This ensures uniformity and simplifies the learning process for everyone.

For those who do not have a Mac, you are still welcome to join. Please be aware that setup steps and certain commands may differ slightly.

### Minimum Laptop Specifications
- **Processor**: Intel Core i5 (or equivalent AMD) or better
- **Memory**: 8GB RAM (16GB recommended)
- **Storage**: 256GB SSD or larger
- **Operating System**:
  - macOS: Version 10.15 (Catalina) or newer (Highly Recommended)
  - Windows: Version 10 or newer with WSL2
  - Linux: Ubuntu 20.04 or newer
- **Internet**: High-speed connection (5 Mbps+)
- **Audio/Video**: Functional webcam and microphone
- **Browser**: Latest version of Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox

### Software and Tools
- **Development Tools**: Visual Studio Code (or Cursor), Git, GitHub
- **Communication Platforms**: Zoom (for classes), Slack (for discussions)
- **Cloud Services**: Render, Netlify, Pinecone

## 6. Internship Program

### Eligibility & Acceptance
- **Eligibility**: Open to all graduates who complete the program in good standing
- **Acceptance & Withdrawal**: Accept formally and withdraw with a one-week notice if needed

### Structure
- **Duration**: 10 weeks (five 2-week sprints)
- **Weekly Hours**: Approximately 10-15 hours (flexible)
- **Format**: 100% remote (Zoom, Slack, GitHub)

### Projects
Interns work on real production projects with Shimizu Technology:
- HafaGPT (AI-powered Chamorro language learning)
- HafaRecipes (AI-powered recipe management)
- Airport Golf Tournament System
- Client applications

### Meetings & Evaluation
- **Meetings**: Stand-ups, sprint planning, and retrospectives
- **Performance Evaluation**: Based on task completion, code quality, and communication
- **Mid-term and Final Demos**: Present completed work to the team

## 8. Policies and Procedures

### Attendance Policy
- **Expectations**: Attend all classes punctually
- **Tracking**: Attendance monitored via check-in
- **Unexcused Absences**: More than three unexcused absences may result in dismissal without refund

### Code of Conduct
Uphold respect, integrity, and professional behavior throughout the program. See the full Code of Conduct document for details.

### Refund Policy
- **Non-Refundable Deposit**: $500 deposit is required and non-refundable
- **Full Refund Eligibility**: Full tuition (minus deposit) is refundable if a student withdraws before the start of the second week of live classes
- **No Refunds**: After the second week of live classes, no refunds will be provided

### Academic Integrity
All work submitted must be your own unless collaboration is explicitly permitted. AI tools are permitted with understanding and proper citation.
