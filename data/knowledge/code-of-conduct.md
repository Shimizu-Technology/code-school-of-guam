# Code School of Guam - Code of Conduct

## 1. Purpose

Code School of Guam (CSG) is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for all participants—students, instructors, staff, and guests. This Code of Conduct sets forth our expectations for acceptable behavior and outlines the steps for reporting and handling unacceptable behavior.

## 2. Expected Behavior

- **Respect**: Treat all community members with courtesy and consideration.
- **Inclusion**: Promote a learning environment free of discrimination and harassment.
- **Collaboration**: Support and help one another. Offer constructive feedback.
- **Professionalism**: Communicate in a respectful manner, whether in-person, via email, or in Slack channels.

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior

- **Harassment or Discrimination**: Offensive comments or actions related to race, ethnicity, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, religion, or any protected status.
- **Intimidation or Bullying**: Deliberate intimidation, stalking, following, or repeated disruption of someone's work.
- **Inappropriate Language or Content**: Including but not limited to sexual or violent language or imagery in any form.
- **Plagiarism/Cheating**: Presenting someone else's work or ideas as your own.
- **Disrespect**: Personal attacks, insults, or other conduct that fosters a negative or uncomfortable environment.

## 4. Reporting Violations

- **Confidentiality**: Individuals who wish to report a violation should do so confidentially by contacting the instructor or program director.
- **Investigation**: All complaints will be taken seriously, investigated promptly, and treated as confidentially as possible.
- **Resolution**: If Code of Conduct violations are found, actions may range from a warning to dismissal from the program without refund, depending on the severity.

## 5. Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all program spaces—online or offline—including Zoom sessions, Slack channels, email communication, in-person events, or any other medium related to Code School of Guam activities.

## 6. Acknowledgement

By participating in Code School of Guam, you agree to uphold this Code of Conduct. We appreciate your help in creating a safe, welcoming, and inclusive environment!
