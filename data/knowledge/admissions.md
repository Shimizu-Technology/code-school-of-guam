## Admission Requirements

### No Prior Experience Required
- Complete beginners are welcome
- No coding experience necessary
- Our 5-week pre-work phase brings everyone up to speed

### What We Look For
- **Commitment**: Willingness to dedicate 12-15 hours per week
- **Motivation**: Genuine interest in learning to code
- **Growth Mindset**: Openness to challenges and feedback
- **Reliability**: Ability to attend classes consistently

### Technical Requirements
- **Computer**: Mac recommended (Windows accepted)
- **Internet**: Reliable connection for video calls
- **Webcam & Microphone**: For live class participation
- **Quiet Space**: Dedicated area for focused learning

## Program Schedule Compatibility

The program is designed for working professionals:

- **Live Classes**: Monday - Thursday, 6:00pm - 9:00pm (Guam Time)
- **Weekend Work**: 3-5 hours of homework
- **3-Day Weekends**: Friday - Sunday for self-study

## Cohort Size

We intentionally keep cohorts small:
- **Maximum 10 students** per cohort
- Ensures personalized attention
- More 1-on-1 time with instructors
- Stronger peer connections
- Better learning outcomes
